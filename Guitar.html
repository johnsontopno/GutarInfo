Archtop guitar

An Archtop guitar is a hollow stell-stringed accoustic or semiacustic guitar with a full body and a distinctive arched top , whose sound is particalarly popular with jazz, blues, and rockabily players.

Electric guitar

An electric guitar is a guitar that requires external amplification in order to be heard at typical perforemance volumes. it uses one or more pickups to convert the vibration of its string into electrical signals, which ultimately are reproduced as sound by loudspeakers.

Acoustic Guitar

An acoustic guitar is a musical instrument the guitar family. Its strings vibrate sound board on a resonant body to project a sound wave through the air.

Resonator guitar

A resonator guitar or resophonic guitar is an acoustic guitar that product sound by conducting string vibrations through the bridge to one or more spun metal cones, instead of the guitar's sounding board.
